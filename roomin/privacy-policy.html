Privacy Policy

At RoomMe, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and address when you register on our website.
  • Room preferences, booking history, and payment details.
  • Technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and device details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you regarding bookings, updates, and promotions.
  • To ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to facilitate room bookings with property managers.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to your personal data.
  • You can update or delete your information by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of promotional communications at any time.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@roomme.com.
